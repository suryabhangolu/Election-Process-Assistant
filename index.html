Voter Registration Guide

  1. Step 1: Go to the official NVSP portal or Voters' Services Portal.
  2. Step 2: Fill out Form 6 for new voter registration.
  3. Step 3: Upload required documents (Age & Address proof).
  4. Step 4: Submit and track your application status.
  5. Step 5: Receive your Voter ID (EPIC) card by post or download an e-EPIC.

    Voting Rights

    Right to Vote

    Every citizen aged 18+ has the fundamental right to vote.

    NOTA

    Right to register "None of the Above" if you do not support any candidate.

    Secret Ballot

    Your vote is entirely confidential and protected by law.

    Right to Information

    Voters have the right to know candidates criminal records and assets via affidavits.
